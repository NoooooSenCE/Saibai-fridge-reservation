Welcome to the Fridge Reservation System

Our community fridge offers a secure and convenient way to share refrigeration space. You can easily reserve a compartment either online or through a physical sign-up at the community center. Follow the steps below to get started.

Get Started Now: Scan the QR code at the community center or Sign Up Here.

How It Works

Step 1: Sign up using our online registration system by scanning the QR code or clicking here.

Step 2: Select an available compartment and get your digital combination lock code.

Step 3: Access your fridge compartment during your reserved time using the secure combination code.

FAQ

How do I reserve a fridge compartment?

You can reserve a compartment by scanning the QR code at the community center or signing up online through this website.

What happens if I forget my combination lock code?

If you forget your combination code, you can retrieve it through the confirmation email or contact the community center for help.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify your reservation by logging into your account or contacting the community center directly.
